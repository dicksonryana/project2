Guns, Germs, and Netflix & Chill


It is an interesting time to be alive at the least. Some would say we are learning a lot of horrible truths about how the world works in a time of crisis. By analyzing google trends, we can get an idea of how people are reacting (or not) to the changes involved with COVID-19. When people use a search engine such as Google, they are wanting to find more information on the topic. Therefore Google Trends is a good platform to gain insight on public interests and concerns, without the potential bias that is posed when conducting research questions.


Guns

Although there is not a significant effect for the category term gun in either 2019 or 2020, the specific search terms perform exactly as hypothesized. There is no effect for ammo, gun stores, or ar15 in 2019, whereas in 2020 all terms demonstrate significant increases associated with the virus.
